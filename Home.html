About our Cafe

Beginning our journey with a cafe in the heart of Pune, Café Madéline provides an escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. From savoury to sweet, from coffee to tea there is something to satisfy every food lovers soul.With an all women team that creates magic in the kitchen and on the floor, Madéline brings together diverse culinary creatives in a contemporary environment. Come enjoy a swirling mass of whipped cream, the sweet dust of icing sugar and the smell of fresh cocoa powder and immerse yourself in all things Madéline.
